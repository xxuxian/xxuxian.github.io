Hi，I am Xian XU 徐娴

Welcome and thanks for visiting my site! I am a Ph.D. student in HKUST Vislab (since 2020), and majored in Computational Media and Arts, co-supervised by Prof. Huamin Qu and Prof. Aniko Imre in USC Cinematic Arts. I received my Bachelor’s degree of Arts in Directing (Film and TV Producing) and Master’s degree of Art in Studies of Drama (Film and TV) in the Central Academy of Drama (中央戏剧学院).

My research interests include Data Storytelling, Immersive Stortelling in AR/VR and Interactive movies and short videos producing. I like to explore and develop storytelling in transmedia and try to combine with Cinematic Arts. I am a TV and film scriptwriter, participating in TV play Our Glamorous Time as a scriptwriter in 2017. I wrote and published the book I Like To be Independent. I also love movies and travelling, I have traveled to most cities in China and different countries, and I like to record life with cameras.
